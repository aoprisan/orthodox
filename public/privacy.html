Orthodox Calendar

Privacy Policy

Effective 18 May 2026

Orthodox Calendar is a hand-rolled liturgical calendar — feasts of the Church, saints of the day, fasting indicators, and the Pascha-derived moveable cycle. It is built to be useful without asking anything of you. This page is the whole of its privacy practice.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no profile. We do not record your name, your email, your location, your device identifiers, your IP address, or any usage analytics.

Tracking, analytics, advertising

There are none. No analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Segment, no anything). No advertising networks. No third-party trackers. No cookies. No fingerprinting. The app contains no telemetry of any kind.

Network access

The calendar, feast data, saint names, and fasting rules ship inside the app itself and work fully offline. The only optional network activity is loading the supplementary Lives of the Saints (viețile sfinților) prose, fetched on demand from the app's own GitHub Pages origin (aoprisan.github.io). These requests carry no identifying information beyond what any web browser sends when loading a static file.

Permissions

The app requests no runtime permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, location, files, photos, or any other sensor or storage area on your device.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because we collect nothing, there is nothing collected from children either.

Data sharing & sale

We have no data to share, and nothing to sell. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer information about you to anyone, because we never have any information about you to begin with.

Changes to this policy

If anything about the app's data behavior ever changes — for example, if a future version were to add optional sync — this page will be updated and the effective date above will move forward.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be sent to aoprisan@gmail.com. Source code lives at github.com/aoprisan/orthodox.
